I completed my bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Delhi Technological University(Formerly Delhi College of Engineering). I presented my bachelor's thesis on "Hardware Implementation of Biological Neural Networks" working under guidance of Prof.Pragati Kumar.

I have also been associated with technology based startup mLabs Research . We worked on canonical implementation of memristor- 4th passive element, and presented a simple memristor setup.

Currently I am working on Refreshable braille display product at Kritikal Solutions associated with Assistive Tech Lab, Indian Institute of Technology,Delhi(IITD) . This project aims to enusre mass availability of digital media for the visually impaired and deaf-blind community through affordable assistive reading/editing device.
